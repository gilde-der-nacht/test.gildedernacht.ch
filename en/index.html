Startpage Association for card, board
and role-playing games

The Gilde der Nacht organizes game meetings where all players are welcome. To make sure you don’t miss anything, we recommend that you subscribe to our newsletter, then you will receive a reminder email one week before each event.

Public transport

From the Lucerne train station, the location is easily reached in 15 minutes by bus 8 (Würzenbachmatte) or bus 14 (Schlösslirain).

Journey by car

A (fee required) parking garage is 5 minutes walk away. Belongs to the Spar supermarket at the address Würzenbachstrasse 19, Lucerne.
